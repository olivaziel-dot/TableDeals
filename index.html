    Our Story

    About TableDeal

    TableDeal was born out of a simple frustration: Lebanon has some of the most extraordinary restaurants in the Middle East — yet most people only ever visit the same handful of places they already know, because discovering and affording new restaurants feels like a gamble.

    We set out to change that. TableDeal is a membership platform that connects food lovers with exclusive deals at verified partner restaurants across Lebanon — from the rooftop bars of Gemmayzeh to the hidden gems of Mar Mikhael, from the coastal restaurants of Jounieh to the lively scene in Achrafieh.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to make exceptional dining accessible to everyone — and to help Lebanon's restaurants thrive by connecting them with thousands of food-loving members who are actively looking for their next great meal.

    For Members

    With a single TableDeal membership, you get access to hundreds of exclusive deals — real discounts, real savings. No gimmicks, no fake "original prices." Every offer is verified by our team before it goes live. Members save an average of 40% on dining out every month.

    For Restaurants

    TableDeal is a growth engine for restaurants. Our partner restaurants gain direct access to 12,000+ members who are hungry — literally — for new experiences. We handle the marketing, the discovery, and the deal management. You just focus on making great food.

    Based in Lebanon, Built for Lebanon

    We are a Lebanese team, building for a Lebanese audience. We understand the culture, the hospitality, and the passion that goes into every dish served in this country. TableDeal is our love letter to the Lebanese dining scene — and we're just getting started.

    "Good food brings people together — great deals bring them back."

    — The TableDeal Team, Beirut 🇱🇧

    Support

    Help Center

    Find answers to the most common questions about TableDeal.

    How does TableDeal work? +
    You sign up as a member, choose a plan, and immediately get access to hundreds of exclusive deals at partner restaurants. When you find an offer you like, open it to reveal your unique live coupon code and show it to the restaurant staff at the time of your visit.
    Can I share my coupon code with friends? +
    No — and it won't work even if you try. Every coupon code is unique to your account and refreshes every 5 minutes based on your user ID and the current time. Screenshots are useless because the code will have expired by the time someone else tries to use it. Restaurant staff verify codes in real time.
    How do I cancel my subscription? +
    You can cancel anytime from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial periods, but you can use your membership right up until it expires.
    How many deals can I use per month? +
    It depends on your plan. Explorer members can use up to 5 deals per month. Gourmet and Annual Pro members enjoy unlimited deals — you can use as many as you like, as often as you like, subject to each restaurant's individual offer terms.
    A restaurant refused my coupon. What do I do? +
    First, make sure the offer is still valid (check the dates and times). If the offer is valid and the restaurant refuses, please contact us immediately via our Contact page. We take partner compliance seriously and will follow up directly with the restaurant.
    How do I add my restaurant to TableDeal? +
    Click "Get Started" and choose "Restaurant Owner" during registration. Fill in your restaurant details and select a partner plan. Our team will review your application and reach out within 48 hours to complete onboarding.
    Is TableDeal available outside Beirut? +
    Yes! We currently have partner restaurants in Beirut, Jounieh, Dbayeh, Jbeil (Byblos), and we are actively expanding to Tripoli, Sidon, and other districts. Explorer plan members are limited to Beirut. Gourmet and Annual Pro members get access to all of Lebanon.

    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: January 1, 2026

    1. Information We Collect

    When you create a TableDeal account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and billing information. When you use the platform, we also collect information about the deals you view and redeem, your district or location (as provided by you), and your device type and browser for security purposes.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your data to provide the TableDeal service, process your subscription payments, generate and verify your unique coupon codes, and send you relevant deal notifications (which you can opt out of at any time). We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

    3. Coupon Code Data

    Each coupon code generated on our platform encodes your user ID and a time-limited hash. This is used solely for anti-fraud verification. We log coupon redemptions to prevent abuse and to provide you with your deal history. This data is never shared externally.

    4. Data Sharing with Restaurants

    When you redeem a deal, the partner restaurant receives only the minimum information needed to verify your coupon — the deal name and the validity of your code. We do not share your personal contact details, billing information, or account data with any restaurant partner.

    5. Data Security

    We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data in transit. Your password is stored using a salted hash — we never store it in plain text. Payment information is processed through a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway and is never stored on our servers.

    6. Your Rights

    You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you, to correct inaccurate data, or to request deletion of your account and associated data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@tabledeal.lb.

    7. Cookies

    We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to maintain your session. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that sell data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your ability to use the platform.

    8. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify all members by email before any significant changes take effect. Continued use of the platform after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

    9. Contact

    For privacy-related inquiries, email us at privacy@tabledeal.lb or use our Contact page.
